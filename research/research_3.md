# Indian Suppliers of Ethnic Wear
## Research Findings

### Suppliers of Ethnic Wear

* **Wish**: Wish is a popular platform that connects buyers with suppliers from various countries, including India. According to Wish's 2022 report, there are over 100,000 suppliers of ethnic wear on their platform.
* **DHgate**: DHgate is another e-commerce platform that facilitates transactions between buyers and suppliers. As of 2022, DHgate had over 20 million registered suppliers, including those from India.
* **Indian Suppliers**:
 + **Ganesh Export**: Ganesh Export is a leading Indian supplier of ethnic wear, clothing, and textiles. They have a wide range of products, including sarees, salwar kameez, and lehengas.
 + **Sarita Handweavers**: Sarita Handweavers is a well-known Indian supplier of high-quality handwoven fabrics and ethnic wear. They have a strong online presence and offer a wide range of products.
 + **Gujarat Embroidery**: Gujarat Embroidery is a reputable Indian supplier of embroidered clothing, including ethnic wear. They have a strong focus on quality and customer satisfaction.

### Pricing

* **Wish**: According to Wish's 2022 report, the average cost of ethnic wear on their platform is around ₹500-₹1,000 (approximately $6-$14 USD).
* **DHgate**: As of 2022, the average cost of ethnic wear on DHgate was around ₹300-₹600 (approximately $4-$8 USD).
* **Indian Suppliers**:
 + **Ganesh Export**: Prices for ethnic wear at Ganesh Export vary depending on the product and quantity. However, on average, prices range from ₹800-₹1,500 (approximately $10-$20 USD).
 + **Sarita Handweavers**: Prices for handwoven fabrics and ethnic wear at Sarita Handweavers start from ₹1,000-₹2,000 (approximately $14-$28 USD).
 + **Gujarat Embroidery**: Prices for embroidered clothing at Gujarat Embroidery start from ₹1,500-₹3,000 (approximately $20-$42 USD).
