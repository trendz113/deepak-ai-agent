# Research on Top 10 Competitors in the Chosen Niche and their Pricing Strategies in India

## Competitor Research Methodology

For this research, we used the following methodology:

* Identified top e-commerce platforms in India using Google Trends, Alexa, and SimilarWeb
* Analyzed their product offerings, pricing, and customer demographics
* Collected data from publicly available sources such as their websites, social media, and news articles
* Used tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, and Moz to gather insights on their online presence and marketing strategies
