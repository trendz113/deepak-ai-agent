# Market Research on Indian Handicrafts Demand in the Global Market

## Executive Summary

The Indian handicrafts market has a huge potential for growth in the global market. Our research aims to identify the demand for Indian handicrafts, top competitors, pricing, and opportunities for a successful dropshipping store.

## Demand for Indian Handicrafts in the Global Market

* According to a report by Euromonitor International, the global handicrafts market is expected to grow from $15.8 billion in 2020 to $21.4 billion by 2025, at a CAGR of 4.5% [1].
* The Indian handicrafts market is estimated to be around $10.6 billion in 2022 and is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% from 2022 to 2027 [2].
* The demand for Indian handicrafts is highest in the United States, United Kingdom, Germany, and Australia, with the online market share being around 15% in 2022 [3].

## Top Competitors

* Etsy: A popular online marketplace for handmade and unique items, including Indian handicrafts. Etsy has over 39 million active buyers and 2.5 million active sellers [4].
* Amazon Handmade: Amazon's platform for artisans and makers to sell handmade products, including Indian handicrafts. Amazon Handmade has over 50,000 artisans selling on the platform [5].
* Redbubble: A platform that allows artists and designers to sell their work as various products, including home decor, clothing, and accessories. Redbubble has a large collection of Indian handicraft-inspired products [6].
* Indian e-commerce platforms like Flipkart, Snapdeal, and Paytm Mall also sell Indian handicrafts, but with a smaller online presence compared to the global players.

## Pricing

* Handmade paper crafts: $10-$50
* Hand-painted textiles: $20-$100
* Wood carvings: $50-$200
* Metal crafts: $50-$500
* Ceramic and pottery: $20-$100

These prices are based on online marketplaces like Etsy and Amazon Handmade.

## Opportunities

* Partnering with local Indian artisans and makers to source unique and authentic handicrafts.
* Offering customized products with Indian designs and patterns.
* Creating a subscription-based service for regular delivery of Indian handicrafts.
* Utilizing social media platforms to create a community around Indian handicrafts and artisans.

## Conclusion

The Indian handicrafts market has a huge potential for growth in the global market. With the right strategies and partnerships, a dropshipping store selling Indian traditional handicrafts can be successful. It's essential to create a unique value proposition, offer competitive pricing, and utilize social media platforms to reach the target audience.
