**Pricing Strategies in Indian E-commerce Market:**

* **Discount-based pricing**: Offer discounts and cashback offers to attract customers
* **Competitive pricing**: Offer prices similar to competitors to maintain market share
* **Premium pricing**: Offer premium products at higher prices to attract high-end customers
* **Dynamic pricing**: Adjust prices in real-time based on demand and supply

**Opportunities in Indian E-commerce Market:**

* **Growing demand for electronics and gadgets**: India is expected to see a significant growth in demand for electronics and gadgets in the next few years
* **Increasing online shopping penetration**: Online shopping is becoming increasingly popular in India, with more and more Indians shopping online
* **Competitive market**: The e-commerce market in India is highly competitive, with multiple players vying for market share
* **Opportunities for niche players**: With the growing demand for electronics and gadgets, there is an opportunity for niche players to establish themselves in the market
